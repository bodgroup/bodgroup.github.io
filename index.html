About us

We are a group of lay Buddhists and people interested in Buddhism who meet to meditate and discuss Buddhist practice at Amaravati Buddhist Monastery on Wednesday evenings at 7.30pm to 9.20pm. We welcome anyone who is interested in Buddhism, including those who are new to Buddhism or new to Amaravati. Our discussions focus on understanding the Buddhist teachings and how we can apply them in our lives. We meet in the Bodhinyana Hall.
